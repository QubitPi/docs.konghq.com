Konnect

Konnect allows you to pay only for what you use with flexible pay-as-you-go billing. There are three plugin tiers categorized by pricing models:

  • Free tier: A selection of our world-class open-source plugins available in Konnect for free.
  • Paid tier: Plugins developed by Kong that are available for a monthly fee.
  • Premium tier: Advanced plugins that are available for a monthly fee.

For more information, review the billing page.
